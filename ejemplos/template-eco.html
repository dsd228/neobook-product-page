Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a good credit score for solar?

We work with customers of varying credit profiles. Our financing options are flexible and designed to work for different financial situations.

How long does installation take?

Most installations are completed within 1-3 days. Our professional team handles everything from start to finish.

What happens if my roof needs repairs?

We handle roof repairs before installation at no extra cost. Your home is fully protected during the solar installation process.

Yes! With our leases, we can relocate your system. With ownership options, the panels are yours to take with you.
